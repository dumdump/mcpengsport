Help Page

Objectives

The objective is to stay alive as long as possible. Zombies will try to take down McPeng Sports, but you will have to protect it.


Controls

'WASD' for movement, 'E' for shooting, 'Q' for 'IN DEVELOPMENT'. You can use your keyboard or the buttons on the screen. (unless peng is dumb and forgot to make them.) ARROW KEYS DONT WORK.


Other Info

If a zombie hits McPeng Sports, it will inflict between 0 - 25 damage. Eliminating 10 zombies will result in a level up. Levels increase zombie speed. Only one zombie spawns at a time (for now).
